About Me

I'm Billy Brown, a young developer from Belgium, currently in my third year of study of a 5-year masters Computer Science course at St Andrews University, Scotland. My topics of study include systems programming, computational complexity, artificial intelligence, distributed systems and more, lending me both knowledge and experience in many fields of Computer Science.

I am currently a Databases and IT Intern at the University of St Andrews Development Office, where I build .NET web applications with Microsoft C#, ASP and SQLServer.

A notable personal project of mine is cropper.js, a small embeddable JavaScript application for interactively cropping images in the browser. It was originally developed for my internship but the open source project will be updated for better portability and embeddability after the current university term.

I try to maintain a strong internet presence on Twitter and GitHub, where you can hear of and take a look at my various projects.

I'm Billy Brown, a young developer from Belgium, currently in my fifth year of study of a 5-year masters Computer Science course at St Andrews University, Scotland. My topics of study include systems programming, computational complexity, artificial intelligence, computer security and more, lending me both knowledge and experience in many fields of Computer Science.

I am currently a Databases and IT Intern at the University of St Andrews Development Office, where I build .NET web applications with Microsoft C#, ASP and SQLServer.

Last summer (June-August 2017) I worked on my project for the The Laidlaw Scholarship Programme in Research and Leadership. Working in constraint programming with Essence, my task was to add a specification strengthening feature to Conjure: The Automated Constraint Modelling Tool, which infers tighter variable domains and new constraints without losing solutions. I applied the tool to the problems on csplib, improving their solve time in some cases. This is also my first major contribution to open source software.

I try to maintain a visible internet presence on Twitter and GitHub, where you can hear of and take a look at my various projects.

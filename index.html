About Me, Billy Brown

I'm Billy Brown, a young web-designer and developer from Belgium, currently starting my third year of study of a 5-year masters Computer Science course at St Andrews University, Scotland, where I study systems and network programming, web-development, artificial intelligence and more, lending me both knowledge and experience in many fields of Computer Science.

I have 5 years of experience in web-development and 4 years in desktop programming, and have recently started paid work for building and running websites, as well as general technology consultancy.

The actual design of a website is not my area of expertise; I prefer to implement designs both on the front- and back-end, using skills I have taught myself over the years, or learned more recently here at university.

I try to maintain a strong internet presence on Twitter and GitHub, where you can hear of and inspect my different projects.

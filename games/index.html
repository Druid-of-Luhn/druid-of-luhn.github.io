Games

I have made a few games for hackathons and for my university course:

  • CityMayor - a city building simulation game (Processing).
  • AsterRift - a VR asteroid shooter with motion controls (Unity, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion).
  • Gundabad - a mouse-controlled top-down dungeon crawler RPG (Processing).
  • SpellOps-4 - a 2d brawl game with wizards (Unity).
  • Artillery - a 2d artillery game with blocky terrain and wind (Processing).
  • TweetX - a 2d space fighting game controlled by Twitter (JavaScript, Python, Twitter).
